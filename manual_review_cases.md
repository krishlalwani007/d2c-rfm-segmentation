# Manual Review Cases

The following customer types require manual review:

1. High spend but high return rate.
2. High sessions but increasing recency.
3. Frequent purchases with many support tickets.
4. High campaign engagement but declining orders.
5. Low spend with excellent ratings.
6. New customers with very few orders.
7. Customers with high abandoned carts.
8. Customers with negative support sentiment.
9. Customers with fluctuating purchase frequency.
10. Customers with high category diversity but low recent engagement.

Business teams should investigate these customers before making retention decisions.
