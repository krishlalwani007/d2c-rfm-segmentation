# Retention Strategy

## Champions

Characteristics:

* High monetary value
* Low recency
* High frequency

Strategy:

* VIP rewards
* Early access to products
* Premium loyalty benefits

---

## Loyal Customers

Characteristics:

* Frequent purchases
* High engagement

Strategy:

* Loyalty points
* Personalized recommendations

---

## At Risk Customers

Characteristics:

* Previously active
* High recency
* Declining engagement

Strategy:

* Personalized offers
* Re-engagement email campaigns

---

## Dormant Customers

Characteristics:

* Low sessions
* Low frequency
* Long inactivity

Strategy:

* Win-back campaigns
* Limited-time discounts

---

## High Value but Unhappy

Characteristics:

* High spend
* High return rate
* Many support tickets

Strategy:

* Dedicated customer success support
* Complaint resolution
* Service recovery offers

## Budget Prioritization

Priority order:

1. High Value but Unhappy
2. At Risk Customers
3. Champions
4. Loyal Customers
5. Dormant Customers
